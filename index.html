Server Rules

No Griefing

Respect other players' builds and creations. Griefing will result in immediate ban.

Be Respectful

Use appropriate language and treat all players with respect in chat and voice channels.

No Exploits

Using bugs, glitches, or exploits is strictly prohibited and will result in permanent ban.

Fair Play

Play fairly and follow all Minecraft EULA guidelines. No cheating or unfair advantages.

Community First
